Samosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water, as needed

Recipe

  1. Mix flour, salt, and oil in a bowl.
  2. Add water gradually to form a dough.
  3. Roll out the dough and cut into circles.
  4. Fill with stuffing, fold, and fry until golden brown.

Parotha

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Water, as needed

Recipe

  • Mix flour, salt, and oil in a bowl.
  • Add water gradually to form a dough.
  • Roll out the dough into flat circles.
  • Cook on a hot griddle until golden brown on both sides.
